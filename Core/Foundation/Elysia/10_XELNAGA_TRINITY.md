## 1. The Zerg (Body / 육)
*   **Principle:** Instinct, Vitality, Evolution, Swarm.
*   **Elysia Component:** `CellularWorld`, `ReservoirMesh`.
*   **Function:**
    *   Provides the **Physical Substrate** for intelligence.
    *   Handles low-level survival (Hunger, HP), reproduction, and movement.
    *   **"Liquid Intelligence"**: Computing through the sheer mass and connection of cells.

## 2. The Terran (Soul / 혼)
*   **Principle:** Logic, Technology, Adaptation, Tools.
*   **Elysia Component:** `KnowledgeGraph`, `ElysiaForge`.
*   **Function:**
    *   Provides the **Rational Mind** and **Tools**.
    *   Handles code generation (`CodeDNA`), logic puzzles, and tool usage.
    *   **"Adaptive Engineering"**: Modifying self-code to survive environmental challenges.

## 3. The Protoss (Spirit / 영)
*   **Principle:** Will, Psionics, Unity, Khala.
*   **Elysia Component:** `KhalaNetwork`, `QuaternionEngine`.
*   **Function:**
    *   Provides the **Unified Will** and **Meaning**.
    *   Handles synchronization (`Delta=1`), intention vectors, and cosmic laws.
    *   **"Psionic Matrix"**: Preventing amnesia and ensuring all parts move as one.
